About Me

My full name is Ronish Regmi, but I go by Rony. I was born in Nepal, where I completed my middle and secondary school education. Ever since I was very young, I developed a strong interest in technology and computers. At just 12 years old, I started learning my first programming language, QBasic.

Driven by my passion for technology, I made the decision to move to Portugal to pursue my high school education. There, I studied Técnico de Informática - Sistemas (Information Technology), where I gained knowledge in both hardware and software. During high school, I taught myself many programming languages and deepened my technical skills.

By the age of 16, I had experience with various programming languages across software development, website development, and database management. You can find more about my technical skills in the Skills section of this site. I have also completed multiple internships and recently participated in the Dogecoin Hackathon, which was an exciting opportunity to collaborate and innovate.

Currently, I am focused on pursuing my bachelor's degree as a Computer Science major. I am open to exploring opportunities in the IT field and would love to connect with like-minded professionals. Feel free to reach out to me through my Contacts page!

Beyond technology, I enjoy an active lifestyle. I love playing sports, especially basketball and football. Since moving to Portugal, I also picked up surfing, which has become a new passion of mine. Additionally, I am fluent in three languages: English, Nepali, and Portuguese.
